Bruschetta

A quick, easy, and healthy appetizer. The freshness is unbeatable!

Ingredients Needed

  • Baguettte, cut into slices.
  • Fresh Mozzerella.
  • Tomatoes.
  • Fresh Basil.
  • Balsalmic Vinegar.
  • Olive Oil.
  • Pecorino Romano Cheese.
  • Salt and Pepper.

Cooking Directions

  1. Slice and toast the baguette in hot olive oil.
  2. Top with sliced tomato.
  3. Season tomato with salt and pepper.
  4. Top with fresh mozerella.
  5. Top with fresh basil.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and balsalmic vinegar, and then a dusting of pecorino romano.
  7. Enjoy!
