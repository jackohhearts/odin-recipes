Hummus

A delicious and healthy hummus recipe to get you through a tough week!

Ingredients Needed

  • One can of chickpeas
  • Garlic
  • Tahini
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt
  • Ice Cold Water
  • Olive Oil

Cooking Directions

  1. Drain the canned chickpeas.
  2. In a blender, blend three cloves of garlic with around 3-4 tablespoons of Tahini for 30 seconds.
  3. Add the drained chickpeas, with a dash of salt on top.
  4. Add approximately 2-3 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice.
  5. Blend until smooth, while slowly adding ice cold water until the desired consistency is reached.
  6. Service with olive oil, pita bread, pita chips, vegetables, anything you'd like!
